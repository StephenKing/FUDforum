Mailing List & NNTP Integration

FUDforum includes automatic tools that allow the forum to be used to archive newsgroups (NNTP) and mailing lists. In addition to archival abilities FUDforum also allows the administrator to permit forum members to post messages back to the mailing list or newsgroup, thus making FUDforum an ideal access point to those resources for people who for whatever reason cannot access newsgroups or mailing lists.
